WHY GITABITAN

Luxury Living

Inspired by Heritage

Established in 2020 in Kolkata, West Bengal, Gitanjali Realty is committed to redefining modern living with premium bungalows and studio apartments in the serene cultural haven of Santiniketan. Our mission is to blend timeless design with sustainable development, creating spaces where families thrive and communities grow. With a strong focus on quality, transparency, and customer trust, we don’t just deliver houses — we craft homes that inspire pride and belonging for generations to come.
